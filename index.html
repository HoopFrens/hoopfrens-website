Our Mission

Built for the Stories
That Deserve to Be Told

Hoop Frens was created to give visibility to the thousands of players, coaches, and programs across college basketball that deserve recognition. We believe every level has great stories, valuable opportunities, and passionate communities.

From the JUCO gym in East Texas to the DIII arena in New England — we show up for all of it. We are not here to rank one level against another. We are here because we genuinely love the game at every level. And while we may occasionally cover Division I news, our heart belongs to the programs and athletes that mainstream media too often overlooks.
